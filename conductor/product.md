# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
The official web platform for the Purdue University IEEE Student Branch, serving as a modern informational hub for technical committees, leadership, and resources.

## Vision
To provide a high-performance, visually engaging, and easily maintainable platform that showcases the Purdue IEEE community to students, members, and corporate partners.

## Target Audience
- **Prospective Members:** Students interested in technical committees, events, and professional development.
- **Current Members:** Active IEEE members seeking project information, governance documents, and announcements.
- **Corporate Partners:** Companies looking to sponsor or collaborate with the branch and its committees.

## Core Goals
1.  **Committee Showcase:** Provide a comprehensive and dynamic directory of all technical committees and their projects.
2.  **Leadership Visibility:** Clearly display the branch's leadership structure and officer information.
3.  **Dynamic Content Management:** Utilize Sanity CMS to ensure that committee and leadership data can be updated without code changes.
4.  **High-Fidelity User Experience:** Deliver a polished UI with smooth animations, high-performance interactions, and seamless transitions.
5.  **Performance & Reliability:** Ensure a fast, snappy experience with optimized data fetching and responsive interactions across all devices.

## Key Features
- **Committee Directory:** Detailed pages for each committee with descriptions, projects, and contact info.
- **Officer Directory:** A centralized listing of current branch leadership and committee officers.
- **Sanity CMS Integration:** Headless CMS back-end for managing time-sensitive or frequently changing data.
- **Optimized UX:** Skeleton loaders and intelligent data prefetching for near-instant page transitions.
- **Resource Hub:** Direct access to governance documents (Constitution, Bylaws) and other member resources.
- **Responsive & Accessible UI:** A mobile-friendly design built with Radix UI and Tailwind CSS.
